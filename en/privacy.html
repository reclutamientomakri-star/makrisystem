1. Introduction

Welcome to Makri System. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers, partners, and visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our services or interact with our website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone, and company provided in forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data to manage outsourcing services, respond to inquiries, evaluate candidates, and improve user experience on our portal.

4. Security and Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with essential providers (hosting/email) under confidentiality agreements or with clients in personnel selection processes.
